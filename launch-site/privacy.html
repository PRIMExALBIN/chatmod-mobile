Beta policy summary

Privacy

ChatMod Mobile is designed to run live moderation from the creator's phone while keeping backend data limited to account, entitlement, backup, support, and audit needs.

What ChatMod Collects

  • Beta-interest email, optional creator name, optional YouTube channel URL, and optional note when submitted through the launch site.
  • YouTube channel metadata needed to connect the creator or bot account.
  • Moderation settings, commands, timers, rule presets, and cloud backups when enabled.
  • Chat message logs, moderation logs, and runtime events when the creator keeps or syncs them.
  • Support diagnostics, crash markers, and opt-in usage analytics for reliability.

What ChatMod Does Not Collect

  • Google passwords.
  • Private messages outside the connected YouTube Live chat workflow.
  • Unrelated contacts, photos, files, or location.

Creator Controls

Creators can disconnect YouTube, stop the foreground bot service, wipe local data, export account/log data, delete backups, and request account deletion. Before public store launch, publish the reviewed legal version at this URL.
