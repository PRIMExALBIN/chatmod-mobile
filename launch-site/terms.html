Beta policy summary

Terms

ChatMod Mobile helps creators operate a YouTube Live moderation bot from their own Android phone. Creators remain responsible for their streams, YouTube account permissions, and moderation decisions.

Acceptable Use

  • Use ChatMod for legitimate moderation, commands, timers, support, and account controls.
  • Do not use ChatMod to harass viewers, evade YouTube enforcement, bypass rate limits, or automate deceptive behavior.
  • Keep the bot channel authorized and configured according to YouTube's current policies.

Service Limits

Phone-hosted moderation depends on Android background behavior, network reliability, YouTube APIs, and creator configuration. A foreground service improves reliability but does not guarantee 24/7 background execution.
