# Storyboard

## Beat 1: No Desktop Babysitter

Timing: 0.0-3.8 seconds.
Narration: "Your stream should not need a desktop babysitter."
Visual: White control-room canvas. Chat pressure builds as message rows sweep in from the left. A large headline lands. The ChatMod mark enters as the calm counter-signal.
Motion: Live dot pulses, chat rows drift, headline rises in, mark scales in.
Transition out: cobalt push panel sweeps right to left.

## Beat 2: Phone Command Center

Timing: 3.8-8.0 seconds.
Narration: "ChatMod Mobile runs your YouTube Live moderation bot from the phone in your hand."
Visual: Large Android phone UI reveals the dashboard: ChatMod Mobile title, running status band, stream name, live chat ID, sync chip, and a start/stop control.
Motion: Phone rises and tilts slightly. Status band and chips enter with staggered y/scale moves. Signal arcs animate behind the phone.
Transition out: push slide, with the queue panel becoming the next scene's anchor.

## Beat 3: Rules Catch The Mess

Timing: 8.0-12.5 seconds.
Narration: "Start it, watch the queue, enforce filters..."
Visual: Moderation queue rows appear inside the phone while a rule profile rail lights up blocked terms, links, caps, repeats, and member bypass.
Motion: Queue items arrive one by one. A delete action compresses a flagged row and turns it into a logged action. Rule pills sweep on.
Transition out: vertical cobalt panel wipe.

## Beat 4: Automation And Honest Sync

Timing: 12.5-16.8 seconds.
Narration: "...send commands and timers, and keep logs, backups, and support diagnostics synced."
Visual: Phone shifts to commands/timers/logs. Supporting panels show cloud backup, local Room logs, WorkManager queue, and support diagnostics.
Motion: Command/timer tiles enter as stepped cards. Sync pulses travel from phone to backend chips. Log rows scroll gently.
Transition out: overexposure flash into final.

## Beat 5: Product Promise

Timing: 16.8-20.0 seconds.
Narration: "Your channel. Your bot. Live chat handled."
Visual: Mark, product name, and final promise on a clean white canvas with a compact phone silhouette behind.
Motion: Mark scales in, words arrive in three beats, green live dot pulses, then a gentle final fade.
