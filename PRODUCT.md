# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ChatMod Mobile is for YouTube creators, streamers, and channel teams who moderate live chat while streaming from a lightweight setup. They may not have a desktop production rig open, and they need a phone-hosted bot that can stay online, respond quickly, and carry their own channel identity instead of a third-party bot name.

## Product Purpose

ChatMod Mobile lets creators run a custom YouTube Live moderation bot from their phone. It connects a channel, detects the active live chat, runs moderation rules locally, syncs protected settings to a backend, and gives the creator clear control over commands, timers, filters, logs, and subscription entitlement.

Success means a creator can start a stream, open the app, verify that the bot is live, and trust it to handle routine moderation without feeling like they are using a generic Streamlabs clone.

## Brand Personality

Bold, capable, direct, and calm under pressure. The product should feel like a serious live operations tool that still has energy and character. It should be confident enough to look custom, but practical enough that a tired creator can use it mid-stream.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI dashboard shells with empty cards, vague gradients, and fake metrics.
- Streamlabs-style naming or branding that makes the bot feel rented instead of owned.
- Landing-page first product layouts when the user needs the actual app.
- Over-decorated moderation screens where style slows down live decisions.
- Dark navy, purple-blue, beige, or orange-heavy palettes that read as template defaults.

## Design Principles

- Live state first: every screen should make the bot status, stream connection, and moderation queue obvious.
- Phone as command center: flows must work one-handed and recover cleanly after app backgrounding.
- Custom identity, not rented bot: naming, profiles, and commands should reinforce that this is the creator's bot.
- Expressive where it helps: draw from the latest Gemini app and Material 3 Expressive for motion, shape, and energy, but use expression to clarify state.
- Full-stack honesty: never fake production readiness. Show real setup states, errors, sync status, entitlement, and YouTube API limits.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast or better for app UI and admin/support surfaces. Support dynamic type, reduced motion, color-blind safe status indicators, touch targets of at least 48 dp on Android, screen-reader labels for controls, and non-color text or icon cues for destructive moderation actions.
